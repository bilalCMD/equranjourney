Your Questions

Answers at a Glance

Serving students worldwide since 2007, with over 16 years of experience in one-on-one online Quran education.

What does the free trial include?+
Our 7-day free trial gives you 3 completely free classes with a qualified tutor. There's no payment, no card required and no obligation to continue. It's the perfect way to experience our teaching before choosing a plan.
How do the classes work?+
All classes are one-on-one and live online, so your tutor focuses entirely on you. Each session is interactive — you read, the tutor listens and corrects, and you progress at your own pace. We also send monthly progress reports so you and your family always know how you're doing.
What software or equipment do I need?+
Just a device with an internet connection (computer, tablet or phone), a headset and a quiet space. Classes are held over Zoom, Skype or Google Meet — whichever you prefer. We'll guide you through the simple setup before your first lesson.
Do you offer both male and female tutors?+
Yes. We have both male and female Ijazah-certified tutors. You can request the gender you're most comfortable with — many families choose female tutors for daughters and sisters, and we're always happy to accommodate.
What age is suitable for these classes?+
We teach all ages. Our Quran for Kids program is designed for children (ages 4+) with fun, patient and engaging lessons, while adults and seniors are equally welcome. Every student is taught at a pace and level that suits them.
What about timings and time zones?+
We operate 24/7 and serve students across all time zones. You pick the times that fit your routine — early morning, after school, evening or late night. Flexible scheduling is one of the biggest benefits of learning with us.
What are the fees and which payment methods do you accept?+
Plans start from $40/month and are billed monthly with no registration fee. The more classes per week you take, the lower your per-class cost. We accept a range of secure online payment methods — see our fees page for full details, and ask us about sibling and family discounts.
Do you offer refunds or a money-back guarantee?+
Yes. Beyond the free trial, every paid plan is backed by a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied within your first month, you can request a full refund — no questions asked.
What happens if I miss a class?+
No problem. If you let us or your tutor know in advance, we'll do our best to reschedule the class to another convenient time, so you never lose out on your learning.
Do you teach Tajweed and Hifz (memorization)?+
Yes. Our courses cover Noorani Qaida, Quran Recitation (Nazra), Quran Translation & Tafseer, Quran Memorization (Hifz) and Advanced Tajweed. Whether you're perfecting your Tajweed rules or working toward becoming a Hafiz, we have a structured program for you.
Is the academy suitable for adults and reverts?+
Absolutely. Many of our students are adults and new Muslims (reverts) starting from the very beginning. Our patient, one-on-one approach is ideal for learning the Arabic letters, correct pronunciation and reading the Quran at a comfortable pace — without any pressure.
How do I register?+
It's easy. Just fill out the form on our contact page or message us on WhatsApp to request your free trial. We'll match you with a tutor, agree on a schedule and you can begin your 3 free classes right away — in shaa Allah.
