Refund & Money-Back Policy

We want you to feel completely confident enrolling with us. Here is our simple, fair and transparent refund policy.

Last updated: 1 July 2026

At eQuran Journey we have built our reputation on quality teaching and complete peace of mind since 2007. This policy explains our free trial, our money-back guarantee and how refunds work, so there are never any surprises.

1. 7-Day Free Trial

Every new student is entitled to a 7-day free trial that includes 3 free classes. There is absolutely no charge for these trial classes and no credit card is required to begin. The trial lets you experience our teaching, meet your tutor and decide if we are the right fit — with no obligation to continue.

2. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you choose to continue after your free trial, your first paid month is protected by a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days of your first paid month, you may request a full refund of that month's fee — no complicated questions asked.

3. How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is simple. Just reach out to us:

  • By email at info@equranjourney.com
  • Or by message on WhatsApp

Please include the name on the account and a brief note about your request so we can assist you quickly.

4. Processing Time

Once your refund request is approved, the refund will typically be processed within 7 to 10 business days. The exact time for the funds to appear in your account may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

5. What Is Non-Refundable

To keep our policy fair to both students and tutors, the following are not eligible for a refund:

  • Classes already taken beyond the 30-day money-back guarantee period
  • Partial or unused portions of a month after the guarantee window has ended
  • Fees for any month other than your first paid month

6. Monthly Billing & Cancellation

Our classes are billed on a simple monthly basis. There is no registration fee and no long-term contract. You may cancel at any time — simply let us know before your next billing date and you will not be charged for the following month.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or need help with a request, our team is always happy to assist. Email info@equranjourney.com or message us on WhatsApp.
