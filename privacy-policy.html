Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we keep it safe.

Last updated: 1 July 2026

eQuran Journey ("we", "us", "our") is an online Quran academy that has served families worldwide since 2007. We are committed to protecting the privacy of every student, parent and visitor. This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect through our website and services, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website or enrolling in our classes, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us, request a free trial or register for classes, we may collect:

  • Your name (and, for children, the parent or guardian's name)
  • Your email address
  • Your phone or WhatsApp number
  • Your country, time zone and preferred class timings
  • The course or learning goals you are interested in
  • Basic technical data such as browser type and pages visited (via analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Schedule classes and match you with a suitable male or female tutor
  • Contact students and parents about trials, classes, schedules and progress reports
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • Process enrolments and, where applicable, billing
  • Improve our website, courses and overall service quality

3. Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors use our site and to improve their experience. We use Google Analytics and Google Ads to measure traffic and the performance of our campaigns. These services may set cookies and collect anonymised usage data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some parts of the site may not function as intended.

4. Third-Party Tools

Live classes are delivered through trusted third-party platforms such as Zoom, Skype and Google Meet. When you join a class, your use of these platforms is also governed by their own privacy policies. We only share the minimum information needed to connect you with your tutor.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss or misuse. Access to student data is limited to staff who need it to deliver our services. While no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, we work continuously to safeguard the information entrusted to us.

6. Children's Privacy

Many of our students are children. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children without the consent and involvement of a parent or guardian. Registration, scheduling and all communication regarding a child's classes are carried out with the parent or guardian. If you believe a child has provided us information without consent, please contact us and we will remove it.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone. We only share data with the service providers required to deliver our classes (such as the video platforms listed above) and where required by law. Your tutor receives only the details needed to teach you effectively.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct or request deletion of the personal information we hold about you, and to opt out of marketing communications at any time. To exercise any of these rights, simply contact us using the details below and we will respond promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please email us at info@equranjourney.com.
