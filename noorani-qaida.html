Course Overview

Start Reading Arabic From Zero

The Noorani Qaida is the time-tested gateway to Quran reading. Step by step, your personal tutor takes you through the shapes and sounds of every Arabic letter, the short and long vowels, and the rules that connect them — until reading words and verses feels natural.

  • ✓ Recognise and write all 29 Arabic letters in every form
  • ✓ Pronounce each letter correctly from its makhraj (articulation point)
  • ✓ Master harakat — fatha, kasra, damma and tanween
  • ✓ Join letters smoothly to read full words
  • ✓ Apply basic Tajweed rules like madd, sukoon and shaddah
