Perfect Every Rule of Tajweed

Tajweed is the science of giving every letter its rightful sound. In this advanced course you go beyond the basics to master the full body of rules in detail — both in theory and applied recitation — until your reading reaches the standard required for an Ijazah, the certified chain of transmission.

  • ✓ Apply ghunnah, idghaam, ikhfaa, iqlaab and izhaar rules
  • ✓ Control every type of madd and the rules of qalqalah
